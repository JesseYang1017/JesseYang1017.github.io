Explore Shipwreck Underwater

This project recreates the experience of exploring a dark underwater shipwreck in a CAVE system. Users take on the perspective of a diver holding a dynamic light source, navigating through an environment where the shipwreck is partially illuminated, and the surrounding areas fade into darkness to mimic the realistic challenges of underwater visibility. I modeled the sunken ship in Blender and built the underwater environment in Unity, using custom materials and shaders to bring the scene to life. The focus was on creating an immersive and visually accurate experience of underwater exploration.
