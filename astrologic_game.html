Astrologic

Link to Project

Astrologic is a class project we created under the constraint of "astrologic reading." Our aim was to design a chill and relaxing game that emphasizes art and music while providing players with a thoughtful puzzle experience. The gameplay involves placing different types of stars on constellation maps, each with specific constraints. Players must deduce patterns to complete the maps and progress.

I was responsible for the entire programming, ensuring every gameplay element was functional and intuitive. This included:

  • Developing star placement mechanics with unique rules and constraints for each star type.
  • Implementing feedback systems to help players understand their progress.
  • Designing constellation map logic, enabling dynamic updates during gameplay.
  • Creating progression mechanics, introducing increasingly complex maps as players advanced.
  • Debugging and refining the gameplay for a polished experience.
